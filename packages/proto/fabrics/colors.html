Colors

Overcoat
A long, warm coat that's worn over other clothing to protect the wearer from the elements. These coats are a staple in both business and formal settings.
  • Usually made from high-quality wool.
  • Collars: Can be single or double-breasted.
    • Raincoat
    A long coat made from waterproofed or water-resistant fabric. These jackets are popular in colder, wetter environments.
  • Provides dry cover from wet elements, Usually made of a tight weave, with a chemical coating on the outside for an effective hydrophobic treatment.
    • Parka
    A heavyweight, insulated coat with a hood.
  • These coats are made of water-repellent material and insulated with synthetic material or down feathers to keep the wearer warm in extremely cold weather.
  • Popular for skiing, hiking, and other outdoor activities.
