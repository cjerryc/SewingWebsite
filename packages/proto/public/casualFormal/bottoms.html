Bottoms

Shorts
A knee-length or less than knee-length pair of trousers.
  • Shorts may be made of many different types of fabrics, depending on the use.
  • Heavier duty fabrics such as denim or thick-weave polyester are suitable for outdoor activities.
  • Acryclic blends are popular for exercise clothing.
    • Trousers
    An outer garment covering each leg separately and usually extending from the waist to the ankle.
  • Trousers range from formal to casual, spanning all different types of fabrics, patterns, prints.
    • Jeans
    A pair of durable twilled cotton cloth used especially for sportswear and work clothes.
  • Jeans are heavyweight and heavy-duty bottoms, suitable for informal or semi-casual wear.
  • The durable material makes this a suitable choice for outdoor activities.
  • Traditionally worn by prospectors and workers, this is also a popular choice for those who opt to keep tools close.
    • Slacks
    Trousers especially for casual wear.
  • Loose fitting and less formal than suit Trousers.
  • May be made of a variety of synthetic or natural fibers.
    • Chinos
    A usually khaki cotton or synthetic-fiber twill of the type used for military uniforms.
  • Cotton twill fabric makes chinos lightweight and versatile.
  • May be dressed up or down.
    • Pairs well with tops.
    Pairs well with coats.
    Pairs well with jackets.
