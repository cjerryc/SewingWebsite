Heavyweight Fabrics

Heavyweight fabrics are defined quite literally as the name suggests. They are heavier fabrics that are more durable, with a tighter weave. These fabrics are typically wind or moisture resistant, less pliable, and hold well when compared to its lighter counterparts. Some examples of heavyweight fabrics are canvas, corduroy, flannels.
