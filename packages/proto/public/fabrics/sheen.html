Sheen

Sheen may be defined as a quality of fabric that has a smooth surface and a shiny luster that reflects light. Sheen may be a subtle way to add flair to formal wear such as dresses, gowns, and evening wear. Sheen is a natural quality of lustrous fibers and preserved by light-refracting weaves.
